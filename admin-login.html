About Us

The Henry LLC is a visionary company located at 10 Court Street in New Castle, Kentucky. This 5,500 square foot commercial space will house a coffee shop, hair salon, and a multi-vendor marketplace. Additional service offerings include office space for local entrepreneurs.

Principal owners: Amy Foree and Joshua Foree. Proposed opening date: April 2026.

Primary sources of revenue include retail space rental, office space rental, and vendor space rental. We plan to open in three phases.
