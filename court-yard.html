The Court Yard

Multi-Vendor Marketplace & Creative Hub

About The Court Yard

The Court Yard is The Henry LLC's multi-vendor marketplace designed to showcase local creativity and entrepreneurship. This vibrant hub provides office space for entrepreneurs, booth space for artisans, and a platform for local artists and creatives to share their work with the community.

Opening Date: TBD (Phase 3)

Concept: Community marketplace celebrating local talent and innovation

Available Spaces

Office Space

Professional office space for entrepreneurs, consultants, and service-based businesses. Flexible terms to support growing local businesses.

Creative Booths

Dedicated booth space for artisans, crafters, and artists to display and sell their work. Perfect for jewelry makers, painters, handmade goods, and more.

Community Event Space

The Court Yard serves as a gathering place for local events, workshops, and community celebrations.
