About Fill My Cup Cafe

More Than Coffee. A Place of Connection.

Fill My Cup is a faith-based counter service coffee shop built on the principle of using quality coffee and genuine hospitality to transform lives and build community.

Our Story

Established by Amy and Joshua Foree, Fill My Cup unites a love for exceptional coffee with a dedication to faith-inspired hospitality. We view coffee shops as welcoming spaces where genuine conversations flourish and lasting spiritual connections are nurtured.

Coming April 2026 to 10 Court Street, New Castle, Kentucky

Meet the Founders

Real people, real passion for connecting faith with community.

Amy Foree

Co-Founder & Visionary

Amy brings a passion for community-centered business and a heart for faith-based hospitality. Her vision for Fill My Cup is creating a gathering space where people encounter genuine connection and spiritual transformation over a great cup of coffee.

Joshua Foree

Co-Founder & Operator

Joshua is the principal operator behind the day-to-day mission of Fill My Cup. His commitment to excellence and hospitality ensures that every customer experience reflects our values of care, quality, and community connection.
